Color Harmony

Introduced 31 May 2026

General Information:

Color harmony describes a balance that Pue, the world, must maintain to remain steady and intact. It dictates the overall presence of color, as well as the ability to control it. Due to large-scale natural operations having a strong reliance on perceivable color as a multi-purpose material, it is crucial that Pue's color harmony can be maintained to a maximum.

Every general setting has a unique chromastone that contributes to Pue's overall color harmony. Chromastones interact with each other in a far-reaching invisible network, where pieces of harmony material may flow freely to survive. Each chromastone is actively maintained to keep its designated position within the network alive. As chromastones thrive off local activity and accumulation of harmony through certain rituals and practices, local overseers (Blanche, etc.) are present at each respective general setting to make sure that everything is in proper order, and that chromastones are fulfilled.

The Ultimate Rainbow Core is a large and inaccessible artifact, located in the center of Pue, that seldomly collects accumulated power from the surrounding network of chromastones. It visually measures the extent to which color harmony is present in the world. Smaller versions of this artifact exist as acquirable charms, which can reflect the actual core's measurement of the world's color harmony in a way that can be seen. Chromastones serve as active beacons to the core, offering sustenance to it without heavy consumption of resources.

When the core is fulfilled after collecting harmony from the surrounding network, all chromastones will experience a year-long buffer where maintenance is not crucial to maintaining a position within the network. During this interval, harmony would continue to flow.

On average, the collection of accumulated harmony occurs once every 10 to 15 years. Because of this, chromastone maintenance by overseers is not particularly aggressive. However, risk can occur when a chromastone is severely tampered with, misplaced, manipulated, etc. Because every chromastone acts as a stable beacon to the Ultimate Rainbow Core, any drastic changes can leave significant effects to its health, regardless of whether harmony collection has occurred.
